FAQ

How do I get started?
Click "Open Bot on Telegram" above, tap START, and use /scan to find your first signals.
Why does /scan sometimes show no signals?
The bot only shows signals meeting quality standards (min 4% EV, 3+ books). When markets are efficient, there may be no +EV opportunities. This is quality over quantity.
What's the difference between A+, A, and B tiers?
A+ Elite = 8%+ EV, 5+ books, <24h (rarest, best). A High = 6%+ EV, 4+ books, <36h. B Standard = 4%+ EV, 3+ books, <48h.
How does auto-settlement work?
After games complete, the bot fetches ESPN scores and automatically marks your signals as Win/Loss/Push. No manual tracking needed.
Is there a free tier?
Yes — free users get 3 scans/day with moneyline markets only. Paid tiers unlock more scans, spreads, and totals.
