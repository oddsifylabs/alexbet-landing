## 🔧 How It Works

### 1. Data Collection
The bot scans The-Odds-API every time you run `/scan`, fetching real-time odds from 11 US sportsbooks across 40+ sports.

### 2. EV Calculation
For each bet, the bot:
- Removes vig from all book odds
- Calculates fair probability
- Compares best available odds vs. fair odds
- Computes Expected Value (EV%)

### 3. Quality Grading
Signals are graded A+/A/B based on:
- EV percentage (higher = better)
- Number of books compared (more = sharper)
- Time to game (closer = less variance)

### 4. Signal Delivery
You receive:
- Summary card with tier breakdown
- Individual signal cards with staking recommendations
- Inline filter keyboard for refinement

### 5. Auto-Settlement
After games complete, ESPN API results automatically settle your signals as Win/Loss/Push — no manual tracking required.

## 🐢 Turtle Doctrine Methodology

AlexBET Signal Pro is built on the principles from **"The Turtle Doctrine"** textbook:

1. **Positive Expected Value (+EV)** — Only bet when math says you have an edge
2. **Market Comparison** — Compare 3+ books to find pricing inefficiencies
3. **Kelly Criterion** — Stake proportionally to edge (capped at 5%)
4. **Closing Line Value** — Beat the closing line consistently = long-term profit
5. **Discipline Over Emotion** — Trust the math, not gut feelings

## ❓ FAQ

### Why does /scan sometimes show no signals?
The bot only shows signals meeting quality standards (min 4% EV, 3+ books, <48h). When markets are efficient, there may be zero +EV opportunities. This is **quality over quantity** — we'd rather show nothing than force bad bets.

### What's the difference between A+, A, and B tiers?
- **A+ Elite:** 8%+ EV, 5+ books, <24h to game (rare, best opportunities)
- **A High:** 6%+ EV, 4+ books, <36h (strong edges)
- **B Standard:** 4%+ EV, 3+ books, <48h (solid, playable signals)

### How do I filter by my preferred books?
Use `/setbooks` to select which sportsbooks you have accounts with. The bot will only show signals available at YOUR books.

### What are A+ Push Alerts?
When enabled (`/setalerts a+`), you get instant Telegram notifications for A+ Elite signals (8%+ EV). These are the rarest, highest-quality opportunities.

### How does auto-settlement work?
After games complete, the bot fetches official scores from ESPN API and automatically marks your signals as Win/Loss/Push. No manual tracking required.

### What is CLV and why does it matter?
**Closing Line Value (CLV)** measures whether you got better odds than the final market price. Consistently beating the closing line = long-term profitability, even before results are in.

### How do leaderboards work?
The `/leaderboard` command shows global CLV rankings. You need minimum 10 signals to qualify. Rankings use a 100-signal rolling average for fairness.
